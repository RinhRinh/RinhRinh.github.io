Our Mission

During the COVID-19 pandemic, science was deemed a low priority and therefore not emphasized in the elementary school curriculum. This has caused severe deficiencies in the subject, especially in children who are now fourth, fifth, and sixth graders. Project STEMkits aims to work with teachers to mend these deficiencies through hands-on projects, a research-based teaching method.
